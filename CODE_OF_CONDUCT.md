# Code of Conduct

## 1. Our Pledge (Біздің уәдеміз)
We are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone.

## 2. Our Standards (Біздің стандарттарымыз)
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Respectful communication
- Constructive feedback
- Collaboration and support

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Offensive comments
- Personal attacks

## 3. Responsibilities (Жауапкершіліктер)
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and enforcing them.

## 4. Scope (Қолдану аясы)
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and public interactions.

## 5. Enforcement (Орындау)
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the project team.

## 6. Guidelines (Нұсқаулықтар)
Warnings may be issued for violations, followed by restrictions if necessary.

## 7. Attribution (Атрибуция)
This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant.
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/
